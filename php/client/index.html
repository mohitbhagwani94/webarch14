Aaruush

Aaruush is the annual techno-management festival of SRM University, Chennai, India. The festival consists of various science and technology based competitions, symposia, events, guest lectures, video conferences, exhibitions, demonstrations and workshops.

Konvolve

KONVOLVE is an Intra-SRM project competition and exhibition exclusively constructed for Multi-Disciplinary Projects, which is open to students under all the faculties of SRM University. The event aims at bringing minds together, "convolving" from various disciplines, to come up with a project, based on their ideas, in the true spirit of learning - a very real time multi-disciplinarily. It gives one's platform to explore one's technical talents one is unaware of?

SRM SCRO

SRM Student Copter Research Organisation is a zealous and vivacious organisation developing copters and their useful outreach under the auspices of SRM University, Chennai.
